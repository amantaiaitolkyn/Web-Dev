Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4-5 people
Ingredients Quantity
cottage cheese 9-20% 200 g
eggs 2
sugar 50 g
vanilla sugar 10 g
butter 50 g
baking powder 8 g
flour 100-120 g

Step 1: Grind cottage cheese in a blender or rub through a sieve.

Step 2: Rub the butter with sugar and vanilla sugar.Add eggs.Mix it up.

Step 3: Add cottage cheese.Mix it up.Add baking powder and flour. Mix it up.

Step 4: Preheat the waffle iron (you can lubricate it with a little oil). Lay out the dough.Close the waffle iron.

Step 5: Bake until golden brown (baking time depends on the power of the waffle iron).

Enjoy your meal!
