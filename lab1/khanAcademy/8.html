Travel to Italy

Recreation options

  1. Tour

    2. The cost of a trip as part of a package tour will cost from 400 to 1000 euros per person per week. Plus a package tour – everything was planned for you, minus a bus, at least 50 neighbors and overnight stays far from the city center, so you probably won't be able to really see all the sights.

  2. By yourself

    3. A little more expensive, but it's much more interesting to plan a trip yourself. There are more than 60 UNESCO heritage sites in the country, dozens of ski resorts, hundreds of beautiful beaches and thermal springs, an endless number of argo farms for rural recreation.

Weather

Italy stretches from north to south for 600 km, the temperature is different, but most likely you will have to undress rather than warm up. In Rome, even in winter, there are a lot of sunny days, the temperature during the day is from +10 to +15 degrees, at night about +5. Snow falls once a decade for a couple of hours and is a miracle for the locals.

Transport: how to get around Italy

It is most convenient to travel between major cities by high–speed trains ItaloTreno and Trenitalia - this is the fastest and most comfortable way to get to Rome, Verona, Florence, Venice, Naples, Turin, Bologna, Milan.
